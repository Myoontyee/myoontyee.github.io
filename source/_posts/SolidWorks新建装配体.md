**Problem Description**：SolidWorks new assembly, engineering drawings can still only open parts, but also prompted the default template is invalid, how to deal with?

**核心思路**：具体定义一下默认模板即可

---




# 设置默认模板

* 打开设置区，点击`工具`→`选项`→`默认模板`→挨个点`...`选模板
